# Copilot Change Watch

## Problem
GitHub / Copilot features can disappear, reappear, or shift across plans and surfaces with little warning. This wastes team time and creates support confusion.

## Why it matters
If teams cannot quickly compare what changed and when, they lose time in screenshots, Slack threads, and guesswork.

## Solution
Copilot Change Watch is a tiny browser tool. Paste your observations, get a concise change brief, and share the result instantly.

## Usage example
Input:
- 2026-03-10 / Pro / Claude Sonnet missing in chat panel
- 2026-03-12 / Pro / Claude Sonnet restored

Output:
- compact change brief
- why it matters
- suggested next step
